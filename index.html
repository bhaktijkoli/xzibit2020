FAQ

What is a “XZIBIT”?

XZIBIT is a national level project exhibition & competition organized by KCCEMSR under the banner of IETE, CSI & IIC. It is a national level event organised for students to showcase their engineering talent in front of the fast-paced world of technology.

Why xzibit ?

XZIBIT is a project competition which will let the students improve their technical knowledge and practical skills while working on a project. XZIBIT is a national level platform in which industry people will visit the stall and may offer direct internships and placement for selected students. Thus it is a Win-Win condition for students.

What is the limit of members in a single group ?

At the most , four members can be in a single group.

Where will the XZIBIT be held and What is the date of competition ?

XZIBIT will be held within the campus of Excelssior Education Society's K.C. College Of Engineering & Management Studies & Research. It will be conducted on 27th of March 2020.

Do you allow walk-ins ?

We will not be allowing walk-ins. To ensure that you’ll be allowed in, please apply ahead of time.

How much is the registration fees per team?

INR 350/- (For Non IETE/CSI Members)

INR 250/- (Minimum 2 IETE/CSI Members in a group)

What is the deadline for registration ?

20th March 2020 is the last date for registering your team. So, what are you waiting for, Register now as the space is limited !

What will the winners get ?

For each category, First prize and Second prize is categorized as 5000/- and 3000/- respectively.
